<p class="eyebrow">About Dara Pressley</p>
<h2>A <em>Living</em> Tradition</h2>
<p>Dara Pressley is an herbalist, spiritual educator, and designer who has spent decades listening — to plants, to ancestors, to the land beneath her feet here in the Pacific Northwest.</p>
<p>She weaves together the teachings of her ancestry with seasonal rhythms and earth-based practice, offering a tradition that is both ancient and alive.</p>
<p>Whether through apprenticeship, workshop, or the zine, Dara teaches with heart, humor, and an unshakeable belief in the power of the plants to help us remember who we are.</p>
